Our Approach

We believe that any student who is going to take their academics seriously must have a vested concern and see how this is going to help them accomplish what they want for their own life. We spend an estimated fourth of the time helping them understand “why” they want to be successful in the areas of a standardized test. We help them understand the test and how it correlates to their success.

We value the student’s personal gain in the educational process and work diligently to combat the mentality of “you need to obtain a score of”; we have learned in our studies that this approach only takes the students so far before they begin to fizzle out. We pride ourselves in keeping parents and staff members on the same page. We have concluded that when everyone is on the same page as far as the student’s success, everyone works harder to obtain the goal (even when it seems out of range).

Our Services

Private Tutoring

We got our start by helping students with their academics outside of the classroom in all areas that are offered in schools. We can assist with multiple subjects and have been able to help students persevere through their difficult narratives, because we are familiar with the standards and the concepts that need to be taught in the classroom.

My High School
Diploma

This initiative when paired with academic support, allows students to set a priority and an “end-goal” for what it is that they want to achieve. The vision for this program was birthed out of the many classes of seniors who were in danger of not graduating. Students have often credited their success to the “My High School Diploma” initiative. We always return the credit and admit, “It wasn’t us, you decided to be successful!”

21 & Up

21 & up initiative is our test prep program tailored to be interactive and differentiated according to the student's academic need. Our model encompasses a holistic framework which is proven to improve students confidence, increases students value for taking the test, which leads to student success on the test.
